1. Join.me
Pros: supports remote access, supports multi-user
Cons: requires a client software to be installed

2.Google Hangout
Pros: supports multi-user
Cons: requires a google account

3. LogMeIn
Pros: secured connection
Cons: requires a paid account (for advance features)

4. Jing
Pros: easy to use
Cons: screenshots you upload has expiration date

5.Awesomescreenshot
Pros: easy to install in browser extension (no seperate software to install)
Cons: could hang your browser

6. CamStudio
Pros: unlimited video/images to capture
Cons: doesn't offer cloud storage

7. Skype
Pros: offers multi-user, privacy
Cons: requires a paid account
