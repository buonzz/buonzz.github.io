3. Avoidance of Mayweather bout. Aside from mayweather, there is no other big fight manny could do. No more big fights means, no more money, bob didnt want that

4. If pacquiao won, again, there will be no other opponent,so in able to gain a rematch, pac should lose. so that there is a rematch, rematch means more money
