For those people who wants 3D movies, well, I got a surprise for you.
YouTube is now offering 3D format on its videos. How?
Well, if you look at the video player, you will notice this "3D" text on the lower right bar
that will appear after you had clicked the gear icon on the bottom

clicking that button changes the viewing experience to 3D

The perfect example for that is this video

Though you wont see the gear icon below ( it is not enabled when embedded to a page like this)
right click the video below
click the "Copy Video URL"
then paste it to the address bar
then you will see, there is the gear icon, and when you click that,
you will see the 3D button

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9jGvCdthQso

BUT, before you jump right to the youtube.com channel,
I might need to said this: 3D format is not available to all videos

Why?, It is because only those with 1080p format is made available for 3D format viewing by youtube

1080p what?

Well, that is another term for Full HD,
again,it is Full HD (1080p), not just ordinary HD (720p) videos

Given that fact, not all youtube videos are in Full HD format
Well, again, this is another step forward to another cool feature in the internet.

Hey, dont forgot to have 3D glasses on the side next time you are going to watch youtube!
